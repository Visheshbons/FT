FT CARS

Driving the Future

FT CARS is revolutionizing the automotive industry by providing cutting-edge technology, new features, and advanced safety systems to enhance your driving experience. Get behind the wheel and drive into the future with FT CARS.

Models

Meet the FT Autopilot, a cutting-edge marvel just released by the innovative brand FT. This revolutionary car boasts state-of-the-art autonomous driving technology, seamlessly blending sleek design with safety features. With its futuristic aesthetic, intuitive interface, and advanced safety suite, the FT Autopilot sets a new standard for the future of driving. It's not just a car; it's a bold statement of innovation.



Meet the FT Box – a cool and blocky hatchback sibling to the Autopilot. Sporting similar cutting-edge features, it stands out with a unique design, including a full roof sunroof seamlessly connected to the windscreen. The FT Box combines style and innovation, offering a distinctive driving experience in a compact, eye-catching package.



Meet the FT Falcon – a revolutionary hybrid with the ability to seamlessly transition from road to air. Boasting a super cool ultra-aerodynamic design, vertical takeoff, and flying autopilot, it's a game-changer in personal transportation. The Falcon shares key features with its terrestrial counterparts, including Hidden AntiGlare™ headlights, a full roof sunroof, and full waterproofing for underwater adventures. With the Falcon, innovation takes flight in a sleek and versatile package.



Meet the FT Flame – a cutting-edge racing sensation that seamlessly shifts between racing and driving modes. With a top speed of 650kph, this supercar showcases an exhilarating design, boasting fire as its exhaust and the distinction of being the world's first Electric + Hydrogen Racecar. The Flame's adaptive speedometer ensures safety, adjusting speeds based on driving modes, while its legendary safety features keep the driver secure. Plus, for a touch of luxury, if fatigue sets in on the racetrack, the Flame takes over in racing mode and brews a cup of coffee for the driver. It's not just a racecar; it's an unprecedented blend of speed, style, and innovation.



Meet the FT Future, an exclusive release in New Zealand that combines futuristic design with cutting-edge technology. With its iconic "M" shaped lights, falcon wing doors, and a full glass roof, this ultra-cool car seamlessly transitions between racing and driving modes. Powered by pure hydrogen, the Future self-refuels through concealed HydroCapture™ vents. Embracing the future of driving, it features full self-driving capabilities, and its keyless entry is managed through a smartphone app. The FT Future is not just a car; it's a bold step into the next era of automotive innovation.



Meet the FT Glow – an experimental marvel available exclusively in New Zealand. This avant-garde car is made entirely of transparent glass, featuring embedded lights for a unique and see-through driving experience. Inside, SoftGlass™ seats offer comfort and transparency, while a curved screen replaces the traditional dashboard. Safety is paramount with a 5-star rating, and the GlassScreen™ technology displays autopilot, adaptive cruise control, and steering assist information on the windscreen using eye-tracking technology.. The FT Glow is a groundbreaking fusion of transparency and innovation, providing a glimpse into the future of driving.
